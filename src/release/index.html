About

The WARREA breakfast meeting is held at 9:00 AM on the third Sunday of every month at Country Waffles, 2300 Athens Ave, Redding. Visitors and non-hams are always welcome.

Association Call Sign: KE6CHO

Echolink Node: KE6CHO-R #390402

Our non-profit organization is a group of amateur radio operators who promote all aspects of amateur radio technology. We are a family oriented organization. Both husbands and wives can be heard on our repeater as well as at the membership meetings. It is not limited to the parents, as we also enjoy having our children involved with the club. Every aspect of amateur radio can be found practiced by some member of WARREA, such as:

  • FM repeaters
  • SSB
  • CW
  • packet
  • digital modes
  • satellite
  • weak signal
  • contesting

Our 145.110 repeater site is hosted thanks to NetsUSA Communications.
